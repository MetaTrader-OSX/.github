## Overview

Professional retail trading requires a platform capable of delivering real-time market data, advanced technical analysis, and reliable order execution simultaneously across multiple instruments and timeframes. <a href="#">MetaTrader</a> has established itself as the global standard for this requirement, running on macOS with the full feature set that has made it the most widely deployed retail trading platform in the world across forex, CFDs, commodities, and indices.

The charting engine is the centrepiece of MetaTrader's technical analysis capabilities. <a href="#">Charts</a> update in real time as market prices move, with nine timeframes available from one-minute bars to monthly candles, allowing traders to analyse both immediate price action and long-term structural trends on the same instrument simultaneously. Over thirty built-in <a href="#">technical indicators</a> cover momentum, trend, volatility, and volume analysis — MACD, RSI, Bollinger Bands, moving averages, Fibonacci tools, and many others arrive ready to apply without any additional installation. The indicators framework is open, allowing custom indicators written in the MQL scripting language to be added and used identically to built-in tools, giving technically inclined traders access to any analytical method that can be expressed mathematically.

<a href="#">Expert Advisors</a> are MetaTrader's automated trading system, allowing strategies to be programmed in MQL and deployed to run against live market data without manual intervention. An EA monitors defined conditions, places orders, manages positions, and applies risk rules according to its programmed logic while the trader focuses elsewhere or is entirely absent from the platform. The Strategy Tester allows EAs to be backtested against historical price data before live deployment, evaluating performance statistics and optimising parameters against real market conditions. <a href="#">One-click trading</a> from charts provides immediate market order execution for manual traders who need to enter or exit positions quickly at current market prices. The <a href="#">order management</a> system handles market orders, limit orders, stop orders, and trailing stops with full control over lot size and risk parameters, connecting directly to the trader's broker account through the platform's native connectivity protocol to ensure that order routing is as direct and reliable as possible during all market conditions.

## Additional Information

MetaTrader's dominance in retail trading stems from a combination of reliable broker connectivity, deep analytical tools, and the open automation framework that Expert Advisors provide. For Mac users who trade forex or CFDs, it delivers the same platform that professional retail traders worldwide rely on without compromise.

The Expert Advisor system transforms MetaTrader from a manual trading tool into an automated trading environment. Strategies that would be impossible to execute manually with the required speed and consistency can be deployed as EAs that operate continuously, following their programmed logic with precision.

The Strategy Tester provides the evidence base for trading decisions that separates disciplined trading from speculation. Testing a strategy against years of historical data before risking capital in live markets is a workflow that MetaTrader supports completely within the platform.
